Medical Disclaimer

Sehat Sathi is a screening tool, not a diagnostic device. It uses computer vision and audio analysis to estimate risk indicators for Parkinson's, Anemia, and Tuberculosis. It does not replace a clinical diagnosis, blood test, or examination by a licensed doctor.

Results shown are probabilistic risk estimates, not medical facts. Ensure good lighting and a quiet environment for best accuracy.

If this tool flags any risk — mild, moderate, or severe — please consult a qualified medical professional for proper testing and diagnosis.
